Sigma Market Season 2
SPECIAL EVENT: "TIME IS MONEY"

Welcome, players.

In this event, you create a new account and all start at one thousand dollars. Over the next 12 hours, a dollar will be removed from your account for every second that you are online. Once you reach zero, your account is DELETED, and you must create a new account and restart. Your goal is to keep spinning and keep your account safe.

Once the event ends, any remaining money in your account is transferred to the main server. Good luck.

Note: Please use the same username as the one on the main server for this event. If you use a different name, no rewards will be given.
